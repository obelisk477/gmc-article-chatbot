   This is step 5 of the vehicle ads onboarding guide.


To show your vehicle ads on Google, you’ll need to link your Business Profile to your Merchant Center account and select a location group. Learn how to link your Business Profiles and Merchant Center account


  Note: All vehicle ads campaigns will be upgraded to Performance Max by September 2023.



Requirements


  Ensure each business listing has a physical address that is valid and current. Learn more
  Ensure the store code assigned to each business listing is unique within the linked Business Group. Learn more
    
      This is to ensure that the correct listing is mapped to the intended vehicle offer(s)
    
  
  Ensure that your business listings are open, active, and have accurate business hours displayed. Learn more
  Ensure the category of the business listing is in the “Store & Shopping” vertical. Learn more



  Note: A Business Profile isn't required if you submit a store feed.
