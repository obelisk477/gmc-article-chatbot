 Add your business registration number


  
    If you’re looking for information about adding your business registration number in the classic Merchant Center experience, click here. 
  



To show Shopping ads in South Korea, you’ll need to have a valid Business Registration Number (BRN), and the prices on your website landing pages will need to appear in South Korean Won.


  Note: Currency conversion is not available in South Korea.


You can manage your BRN in Your Business area in Merchant Center going to the Business details section and editing them. Learn more about providing an accurate Business Registration Number


  
    
      
      In your Merchant Center account, click on Your business  in the left navigation menu.
    
    
      
      
      On the “Business details” card, click Edit business details.
      
    
    
      
      
      Scroll down until you find “Korean business registration number”. Enter your 10-digit registration number.
      
    
    
      
      Click Save.
