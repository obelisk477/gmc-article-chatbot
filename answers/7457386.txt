  This is step 2 of the local inventory onboarding guide.


To show your local products on Google, you must enable local inventory ads or free local product listings in the Merchant Center:


  Sign in to your Merchant Center account.
  Click Growth in the navigation menu.
  Click Manage programs.
  Click Get started on the local inventory ads or the free local product listings program card.
  Choose the country where your physical stores are located.


Merchant Center programs are only visible to Merchant Center account admin users.
