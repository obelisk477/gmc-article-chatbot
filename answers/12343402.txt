 About Agency partners

This article provides details on Agency partners in the LIA Partner Program.

Agency partners provide the solutions for onboarding feeds, campaign management, media strategy, and additional tech solutions.

These partners are a resource for retailers seeking an end-to-end full service solution to help create feeds and provide local inventory ads campaign planning and execution services.
