Resources in this help center

There are some cases when you may need to take action in your linked Merchant Center account, even if you primarily use a different platform. Here are a list of those exceptions:


  Promotions

  
    You may need to use Merchant Center to opt out of promotions. Learn more about managing promotions with third party partners
  



  Policy

  
    If you contact support regarding policy issues or account suspensions, agents may direct you to Merchant Center to make changes in your account.
