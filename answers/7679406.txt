Before you begin

If you have a shipping service set up in Merchant Center, you can either reuse that service or create a new one just for Buy on Google.

Shipping services for Buy on Google

When customers purchase items through Buy on Google, Google will collect any related shipping fees based on your shipping configurations in Merchant Center. The shipping model you configure will be used to display an estimated delivery date and a shipping price to customers, so it's important to model services that reflect your offerings.
